The Role of Electrons

Electrons are the fuel of life. Mitochondria extract them from food, but that process creates metabolic waste ‚Äî mainly positive ions, which are missing electrons.
These reactive ions damage nearby healthy tissue by stealing their electrons, creating what we call inflammation ‚Äî a hallmark of nearly every chronic disease.

The key to preventing this inflammation is twofold:

  • Reduce the number of positive ions produced by the mitochondria.
  • Supply the body with free electrons from non-food sources, so healthy tissues aren‚Äôt sacrificed.
